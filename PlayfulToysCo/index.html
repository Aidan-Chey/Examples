About Playful Toys Co.

Playful Toys Co. has been bringing quality and exciting entertainment objects to little hell raisers since the 1980's. The company's CEO has seen Playful Toys Co. grow to a inter-dimentional entity since it's opening in Eden's Garden.

Our factories in Mordor have expanded 3 fold to meet with the ever increasing demand for our products. In order to maintain our product's quality we have replaced our child workforce with automation, employing the mystical powers of the Underverse. through employment of the Underverse, we are proud to announce that we are 100% environmentally friendly with 0 emissions.
